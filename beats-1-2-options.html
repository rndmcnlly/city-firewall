BEAT 1

The Boring Login

It looks like a normal, dull login screen. The whole joke is that it will never let you in the front door. No matter what you type, you get bounced.
ADead Serious Denial A plain corporate login. Type anything, get a big red ACCESS DENIED stamp. Cold and official, like a real bank or spy agency.

What happens
  • Type a guess, hit Log In.
  • Screen shakes, red "ACCESS DENIED" stamps down.
  • Tries left: 3... 2... 1... it just keeps failing.
  • Feels like a real locked door. Makes the secret way in feel earned.
BSassy Computer The login box talks back and teases you. Every wrong guess gets a funnier insult. Silly and giggly.

What happens
  • Each wrong password gets a new sassy line.
  • The messages get funnier the more you try.
  • Maybe it starts a fake "hint" that's totally useless.
  • Lighthearted, sets a goofy tone for the whole hack.
CLoading Forever It pretends to work, then betrays you. A spinner says "Checking..." then "Verifying..." then crashes to an error. The classic annoying website.

  • Click Continue, watch a hopeful loading bar.

  • So relatable it's funny (every kid knows this pain).
  • The "this thing is broken" feeling makes hacking it satisfying.
BEAT 2

The Secret Terminal Button

There's a hidden way in that the boring login doesn't want you to find. Once you spot it and click, you drop into the hacker terminal and Beat 3 begins.
ATiny Corner Link A small, faint ">_ terminal" blinking quietly in a corner. You have to actually look for it. Rewards the sharp-eyed.
USERNAME
PASSWORD
LOG IN
>_ terminal
What happens
  • A faint blinking link hides in the bottom corner.
  • Hovering makes it glow a little brighter.
  • Click it and the screen flips to the hacker terminal.
  • Feels like finding a secret door. Very "I'm a real hacker."
BDO NOT PRESS A glitchy, wobbling button appears that literally says "DO NOT PRESS." Of course you press it. Maximum mischief.

  • After a few failed logins, a forbidden button glitches in.

  • Press it → "WELL YOU PRESSED IT" → into the terminal.
  • Irresistible to a kid. Pure comedy.
